About BLAMEO

Blameo technology JSC was established on 26/3/2017 by a talent team from the famous university: Posts & Telecoms Institute of Technology, Hanoi University of Science and Technology and Foreign Trade University.

We live for the mission of becoming a global provider of IT solutions and services, always creating innovation value for our customers. We always work with a sense of responsibility, professionalism, efficiency, build sustainable relationships and always aim to become a trusted technology consultant for our customers.

The team of professional engineers, full of ambition and enthusiasm, has participated in many projects in country or abroad will provide customers with quality products as desired.

We are expert in Linux, MacOS, with the technology of JS Stack; professional in PHP, Java, Nodejs, Objective C, Swift, Javascript, Python running on DB of Oracle, Microsoft SQL Server, MySql, MongoDb, Redis, Neo4j.
